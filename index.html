ABOUT US

Our Objectives

To promote welfare, social, cultural, charitable, health, education, and humanitarian activities to improve the quality of human life.

To offer scholarships, stipends and financial support to deserving students in pursuing higher studies in Medicine, Art, Science and Engineering.

To establish and run comprehensive programmes that contribute to creating community consciousness on the need for environment conservation.

To help and collaborate in establishing medical centers, old age homes, and rehabilitation institutions for the destitute.
